# SWARM — Beta Testing Guide

Thank you for helping test SWARM before release. Check TestFlight for the current version — feature-complete for beta, still tuning balance and polish.

## Install (TestFlight)

1. Accept the TestFlight invite email on your iPhone (iOS 16+).
2. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store if needed.
3. Open TestFlight → **SWARM** → **Install**.
4. Launch SWARM from your home screen.

If the invite link expired, ask the developer for a fresh invite.

## How to play

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Move | Touch anywhere and **drag** — floating joystick |
| Attack | **Automatic** — weapons fire on their own |
| Level up | Collect **green XP gems** from kills, then pick 1 of 3 upgrades |
| Meta shop | **Upgrades** on the main menu — spend **cores** earned each run |

**Goal:** survive as long as possible. One life per run. Beat your best time.

## What to test

Please play **at least 3 runs** (short runs are fine) and note:

- **Controls** — Does the joystick feel responsive? Any stuck input?
- **Difficulty curve** — Too easy early? Too brutal after 1–2 minutes?
- **Level-up choices** — Are upgrades clear? Any confusing options?
- **Performance** — Frame drops, heat, or battery drain on your device?
- **Audio & haptics** — Toggle in **Settings** (gear on menu); report if broken
- **Game Center** — Menu shows sign-in status; new best times should post to the leaderboard
- **Share** — Tap **Share** on the death screen; does the card look right?
- **Crashes** — Note what you were doing when it happened

## What to report

Send feedback via TestFlight’s **Send Beta Feedback** (screenshot + notes) or email the developer with:

1. **Device** (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 18.2)
2. **Build** (TestFlight build number)
3. **What happened** vs what you expected
4. **Steps to reproduce** (if it’s a bug)
5. **Screenshot or screen recording** when helpful

## Known issues (beta)

- **Simulator:** Game Center is unavailable; leaderboard submit is skipped (expected).
- **Art:** Flat neon placeholders — final art pass comes later.
- **Balance:** Boss at 1:30, enemy scaling, and meta costs are still being tuned.
- **First-run hint:** Dismissible tip on the menu; won’t show again after you close it.

## Tips

- **Magnet** and **Vitality** upgrades help new players last longer.
- Spend cores on **Overclock** and **Reinforced Hull** between runs for steady power.
- The boss arrives at **1:30** — save aggressive kiting for then.

## Privacy

SWARM stores progress locally: cores, upgrades, best time, sound/haptics preferences, and whether you dismissed the first-run hint. Game Center sends your best survival time to Apple’s leaderboard when signed in. No account is required to play offline.
